LFSR testbench

11/05/2001 - LFSR testbench 1.20 (beta) adds more sequence lengths.
07/05/2000 - LFSR testbench 1.10 fixes the schematic feedback paths when the 'extended-sequence logic' is used. HDL generation was correct and has not changed. Thanks for Rick Collins for bringing that to my attention.

LFSRs are pretty well known and understood. You can find numerous descriptions in the literature and on the net.
My contribution is LFSR testbench 1.20 beta (156KB) software. It will help you understand LFSR basics:

  • Change interactively the LFSR settings (length, taps...).
  • Visualize the schematic.
  • Get the binary sequence list and length.
  • Get the corresponding HDL designs (in VHDL, AHDL and Verilog).

Note that the software always produces non-parameterized LFSR designs, so here's an example of a parameterized one (AHDL).
